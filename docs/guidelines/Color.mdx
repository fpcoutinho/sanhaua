# Color

Colors play a crucial role in our design system, providing visual cues and enhancing the user experience. This guide provides an overview of the color styles used in our design system.

## Color Palette

Our color palette consists of generic, neutral, and semantic colors. Each color category has variations for light and dark themes.

### Light Theme

<br />
#### Neutral Colors

Neutral colors are essential for creating balance and allowing content to stand out. They are commonly used for backgrounds, borders, and typography in our design system.
